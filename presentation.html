2. Execution

Rockwell Collins & Intermec

  • Avionics & FMS Lifecycle: Directed full life-cycle development for AIMS and Flight Management Systems.

3. Transformation

Collins Aerospace & Transamerica

  • Agile Coach / Scrum Master integrating Lean principles and TDD.
  • Shifted focus from building software to building teams.
  • Managed $25M+ lifecycle portfolios and cross-functional teams of 9–15 engineers.
  • Reduced project schedules by 25% while increasing Agile velocity by 2.5x.
